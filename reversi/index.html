Reversi

Othello-style strategy game

5 Skilled
Beginner Mastery

How to Play

Objective

Have the most discs of your color on the board when the game ends.

Setup

The game starts with 4 discs in the center — 2 black and 2 white in a diagonal pattern. Black always moves first.

Making a Move

On your turn, place a disc on an empty square so that it outflanks one or more of your opponent's discs. Outflanking means your new disc and an existing disc of your color form a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) with one or more opponent discs in between.

All outflanked discs are flipped to your color. You can flip in multiple directions with a single move.

Semi-transparent dots show your valid moves. If you have no valid moves, your turn is skipped.

Game End

The game ends when neither player can move — usually when the board is full. The player with the most discs wins.

Strategy Tips

  • Corners are king — corner discs can never be flipped. Claim them early.
  • Avoid X-squares — the squares diagonally adjacent to corners often give your opponent the corner.
  • Edges are strong — edge discs are hard to flip and help you control the board.
  • Mobility matters — having more available moves gives you flexibility. Don't flip too many discs early.
  • Use Hint — tap the Hint button during gameplay to see a recommended move highlighted in gold.
