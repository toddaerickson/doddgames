How to Play

Objective

Be the first to reach the target score (default 5,000) across multiple rounds.

Card Values

Joker50 points
Ace (high)15 points
Ace (low, in A-2-3)5 points
K, Q, J10 points
2 – 10Face value

Each Turn

  1. Draw — Take the top card from the draw pile, OR pick up a card from the discard pile (you must take all cards above it and immediately meld the chosen card).
  2. Meld (optional) — Lay down sets (3–4 of same rank) or runs (3+ consecutive same suit). You can also lay off cards onto any existing meld.
  3. Discard — Place one card on the discard pile to end your turn.

Jokers

Jokers are wild and can substitute for any card in a meld.

Scoring

At round end: add melded card values, subtract unmelded hand cards. The round ends when someone goes out (empties their hand) or the draw pile is exhausted.

Winning

First player to reach the target score wins. If both cross the target in the same round, the higher score wins.
